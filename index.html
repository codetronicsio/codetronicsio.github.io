Meet the team

Luis Bosch

Luis is an Electronics Engineer from the Catholic University of Cordoba (UCC), graduated in 2009. He's always had a knack for computers, which led him to get involved in projects involving technology, both hardware and software, and choose the path that he did. His latest projects have involved full stack development of web applications for real-time data processing.

In his free time, he enjoys practicing and teaching Taekwon-Do and jamming with his metal band.

Santiago Avalos

Santiago discovered programming while making small computer games with some of his high-school friends. He graduated from the National University of Cordoba, with a CS mayor degree, specializing on IA research. He started working on local start-ups as Game Designer and C++ Developer, before moving to Codetronics, where he focuses on web and hybrid mobile applications.

When he's not coding, music is his other passion.

Victor Rosales

Alejandro Paredes
